I'm Rodolfojr — an AI content creator based in Antipolo, Philippines, passionate about blending artificial intelligence with visual storytelling. From educational explainers that break down complex topics to photorealistic cinematic pieces that challenge the line between real and AI-generated, my work explores what's possible when creativity meets cutting-edge technology.

Each video is meticulously crafted using state-of-the-art AI generation tools. I use CapCut to seamlessly combine AI-generated sequences, add precise captions, and edit everything together to achieve the highest level of visual fidelity and narrative impact.

With hands-on experience in prompt engineering, social media content creation, and basic video editing, I bring a strong drive to deliver high-quality digital content that captivates and educates audiences.
